About Us

Welcome to JS Upholstery Shop, where quality craftsmanship meets exceptional service. Founded by Joseph Lynel Martinez and co-founded by Princess Shera Martinez, our shop has become a trusted name in the upholstery industry. With years of experience and a passion for transforming spaces, we specialize in a wide range of services, including custom upholstery, furniture restoration and repair, and reupholstery.

Mission: Our mission is to bring every piece of furniture's story to life through tailored upholstery solutions that reflect each client's unique style and needs, ensuring excellence and satisfaction in every project.

Vision: We envision becoming the premier destination for upholstery services, known for our exceptional craftsmanship, innovative design solutions, and dedication to enhancing the beauty and functionality of every space we touch.

We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and commitment to excellence. Our team uses only the highest quality materials and the latest techniques to ensure that every project is completed to the highest standards. From residential homes to commercial spaces, we offer a comprehensive range of services to meet all your upholstery needs.

Thank you for choosing JS Upholstery Shop. We look forward to working with you to create beautiful, functional pieces that you'll love for years to come.
