Services

Custom Upholstery

JS Upholstery Shop provides custom furniture upholstery services, allowing clients to design and personalize their sofas, chairs, ottomans, and more. Our expert craftsmen work closely with customers to ensure the final product matches their unique specifications and complements their interior decor.

Restoration and Repair

Our furniture restoration and repair services breathe new life into worn-out or damaged pieces. We specialize in restoring antique or vintage furniture to its former glory, ensuring that each piece retains its original charm and functionality while looking as good as new.

Reupholstery Services

Reupholstery services at JS Upholstery Shop transform existing furniture by replacing old fabric, padding, springs, and webbing. This service is ideal for clients who want to refresh their beloved furniture without the need for complete replacement, giving their home a fresh and updated look.
